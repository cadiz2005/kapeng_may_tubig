ABOUT US




Located in the vibrant heart of Quezon City, Kapeng May Tubig offers a tranquil haven where the art of coffee meets the warmth of genuine hospitality. Our shop is designed to be more than just a place to grab your daily caffeine fix—it is a sanctuary where you can escape, unwind, and savor the moment. As you enter Kapeng May Tubig, the inviting aroma of our freshly brewed coffee welcomes you. Our cozy interior, with its thoughtfully chosen decor and comfortable seating, provides the perfect backdrop for your coffee experience. Whether you aree catching up with friends, working on a project, or simply >enjoying a quiet moment to yourself, our ambiance ensures that every visit is special. At the heart of our shop is a dedication to coffee excellence. Our skilled baristas are passionate about their craft, using only the finest beans sourced from sustainable farms worldwide. Each bean is carefully selected and roasted to perfection, ensuring that every cup of coffee we serve is rich, flavorful, and a true testament to our commitment to quality. From the bold, invigorating espresso to the smooth, creamy lattes, every drink is made with precision and care. We offer a wide range of options to suit every palate, including classic brews, innovative seasonal specials, and indulgent treats. Our menu is designed to cater to both the coffee aficionado and the casual drinker, ensuring that there is something for everyone. But our commitment to excellence does not stop at coffee. Our selection of freshly baked pastries, savory snacks, and wholesome breakfast options are crafted to complement our beverages perfectly. Each item is made with high-quality ingredients and prepared with the same attention to detail that goes into our coffee. At Kapeng May Tubig, we believe that every visit should be a delightful experience. Our friendly staff is here to make you feel at home, whether you are here for a quick stop or a leisurely stay. We take pride in creating a space where you can relax, recharge, and enjoy the simple pleasure of a perfectly brewed cup of coffee. Join us at Kapeng May Tubig, where every visit is more than just a coffee run—it is a chance to experience a little bit of comfort and joy. We look forward to welcoming you and making every sip a moment to remember.
